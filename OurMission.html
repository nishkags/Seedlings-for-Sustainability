Our Mission

We visit Bay Area elementary schools and childcare programs to talk to 1st-5th graders about sustainability and donate planting kits for kids to look after. This initiative is designed to engage students with fun, hands-on activities while inspiring them to actively care for their environment.

We aim to empower Bay Area communities through sustainable urban. We provide education, planting kits, and support to foster self-reliance, create healthier environments, and improve food security. Join us on our mission for a greener future!
