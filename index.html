About Us

Seedlings for Sustainability is a community service project based in California that aims to educate the youth about the environment and sustainability, targeting food insecurity at the root by promoting planting. With the help of our partners and local farms like Garden to Table in Silicon Valley, we have created over 150 mini plants out of recycled newspaper and donated seeds. Our plants are easy to maintain and thrive under any condition.
